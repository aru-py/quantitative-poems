\item $C$: \index{Communication}\textit{Communication}. The honest exchange of desires, boundaries, and emotions that creates understanding and safety, enabling partners to express their authentic selves without judgment.
\item $T$: \index{Time}\textit{Time}. The duration of shared experiences and relationship development, appearing as an exponent because intimacy grows exponentially as partners invest more moments together.
\item $R$: \index{Resistance}\textit{Resistance}. Internal barriers such as shame, past trauma, or societal conditioning that create emotional walls and prevent full surrender to intimate connection with another person.
\item $V$: \index{Vulnerability}\textit{Vulnerability}. The fear of emotional exposure and potential rejection that acts as a protective mechanism but paradoxically limits the very intimacy we seek in relationships.}{This equation reveals intimacy as the product of attraction and communication, amplified exponentially by time invested together, yet tempered by our internal resistances and fears of vulnerability.
